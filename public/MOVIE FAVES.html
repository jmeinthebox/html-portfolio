Jaime's Top Favorite Movies 📽️🎞️

Suzume (2022)

This is the 2nd film by Shinkai Makoto that I have ever seen, following "Your Name", which got me obsessed with his work. One of my favorite things about this film is the OST title track, "Suzume", as performed by RADWIMPS ft. Toaka. It really puts me in an indescribible space of imagination. 👫🗝️

Spy x Family: Code White (2023)

This film is the first feature-length film of an anime show I enjoy. There are currently two seasons of this show and it's very entertaining to watch. I always enjoy the facial expressions illustrated in this show. They make me laugh really hard. 🤵🏼👧🏼👰🏻‍♀️🐶

Your Name (2016)

This film is an absolute work of art and is the first of all Shinkai Makoto films I've seen. I've definitely enjoyed Studio Ghibli works such as "Princess Mononoke" — but I resonate with the conceptual nature of Shinkai's work moreover. 👫
